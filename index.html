"Cyclone" Arcade Game

Designed an embedded computer based on the ATmega32 microcontroller to mimic the reaction-based arcade game Cyclone.

How it works:

  • Each LED in the LED array will be turned on one-by-one going left to right and then wrap around.
  • The player will try to test their reaction time by pressing the button when the middle (red) LED is lit.
  • If the player is successful: a happy jingle plays, the LEDs animate, the player wins the jackpot, and the jackpot is reset.
  • If the player is not successful: a sad jingle plays, the player wins a small amount of points based on how close they were, and the jackpot goes up.
